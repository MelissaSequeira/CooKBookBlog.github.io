Chicken Lolipops

Let's see its recipe...

Ingredients:

    For Chicken:

  1. chicken wings
  2. 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  3. 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  4. 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  5. 1/2 teaspoon black pepper powder
  6. 1 tablespoon vinegar
  7. 1 egg
  8. 3-4 tablespoons all-purpose flour (maida)
  9. 3-4 tablespoons cornflour
  10. Salt to taste
  11. Oil for deep frying

    For the sauce:

  1. 2 tablespoons oil
  2. 1 tablespoon finely chopped garlic
  3. 1 tablespoon finely chopped ginger
  4. 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  5. 1/4 cup finely chopped onions
  6. 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  7. 1 tablespoon red chili sauce
  8. 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  9. 1 tablespoon vinegar
  10. 1 tablespoon brown sugar or honey
  11. Salt to taste
  12. Chopped spring onions for garnish (optional)

How to make?

  1. Pat the chicken lollipops dry with paper towels.
  2. Combine the soy sauce, chilli powder, ginger garlic paste, ketchup, and vegetable oil in a large mixing basin.
  3. Add the chicken lollipops to the marinade and toss to coat. Put it covered in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour & up to 12 hours.
  4. Combine the all-purpose flour, rice flour, cornflour, cornstarch, egg, and salt in a large mixing basin. Blend the batter until it is smooth and free of lumps.
  5. Allow any extra marinade to drip off the chicken lollipops before removing them from the marinade
  6. 1/4 cup roasted peanuts
  7. Holding one chicken lollipop's exposed bones, dip the meaty end of the chicken lollipop into the batter to completely coat, then place it aside on a dish. Repeat this step with the rest of the chicken lollipops.
  8. Paper towels should be used to line a sheet tray.
  9. Heat three inches of vegetable oil in a big saucepan or Dutch oven to 180‚ÑÉ.
  10. Fry the chicken lollipops in batches until golden brown, about 5 minutes for each lollipop.
  11. Place the fried chicken lollipops on a sheet tray lined with paper towels. Serve immediately
