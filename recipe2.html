Misal Pav

Let's see its recipe...

Ingredients:

    For misal

  1. Sprouted moth beans (Matki)
  2. Oil for cooking
  3. Mustard seeds
  4. Cumin seeds
  5. Asafoetida (Hing)
  6. Curry leaves
  7. Onion, finely chopped
  8. Ginger-garlic paste
  9. Tomatoes, chopped
  10. Misal masala (a spice blend specific to Misal)
  11. Red chili powder
  12. Turmeric powder
  13. Salt to taste
  14. Water
  15. Farsan (crunchy snack, usually sev or chiwda)

    For Garnish

  1. Coriander leaves, chopped
  2. Onion, finely chopped
  3. Lemon wedges
  4. Pav (bread rolls)

How to make?

  1. Take moong sprouts in a pressure cooker. Add in salt, turmeric and water. Pressure cook for 1 whistle and set aside till pressure releases.
  2. Now heat 2 tblsp oil in a pan, add onions, ginger garlic paste and saute for 2 mins.
  3. Add in tomatoes and cook till mushy.
  4. Now cool this down. Take it in a blender and puree till smooth.
  5. Heat 3 tblspoil in a kadai. Add cumin and curry leaves. Leave them to sizzle.
  6. Add in spice powders, along with little water and cook till oil separates.
  7. Add in ground masala and cook till oil separates.
  8. Now add water, cooked sprouts, jaggery and tamarind. Mix well and cook on low heat for 10 to 12 mins till oil floats on top.
  9. Spoon this in a bowl, top with onions, coriander leaves, sev and serve with pav buns and lemon wedges.
  10. Ready to serve.....
