Schezwan Fried Rice

Let's see its recipe...

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups cooked basmati rice (leftover rice works well)
  2. 1/2 cup finely chopped mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers, beans, etc.)
  3. 1/4 cup finely chopped cabbage
  4. 1/4 cup chopped spring onions (white and green parts separated)
  5. 200 grams boneless chicken, thinly sliced
  6. 2 eggs, beaten
  7. 1 cup of boiled noodles
  8. 2 tbsp Schezwan sauce
  9. 1 tbsp soy sauce
  10. 1 tsp vinegar
  11. 2 tbsp oil
  12. 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  13. Salt to taste
  14. 1/2 tbsp cornflour dissolved in 1 cup of water a pinch of sugar.
  15. 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  16. 1 tsp red chili flakes (optional, for extra heat)
